Privacy Policy - ratio.ai Chrome Extension

Last updated: January 8, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the ratio.ai Chrome Extension ("Extension", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our browser extension.

Single Purpose: Our extension extracts recipe ingredients from recipe websites and processes them into clean, memorable cooking ratios through the ratio.ai service.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Website Content

When you use the extension on a recipe website, we collect:

  • Recipe ingredient lists and measurements
  • Recipe titles
  • Structured recipe data (JSON-LD format when available)
  • The URL of the recipe page (for reference links)

2.2 Local Storage

We may store locally on your device:

  • Your extension preferences
  • Recently processed recipe URLs (to avoid duplicate processing)

3. How We Use Your Data

The collected recipe data is used to:

  • Extract and parse ingredient lists
  • Calculate cooking ratios and measurements
  • Display clean, formatted recipe information
  • Provide links back to the original recipe source

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

4.1 ratio.ai Service

Recipe content is sent to our servers at ratio-ai-kbrobedkgq-uc.a.run.app for processing. This includes:

  • Ingredient lists and measurements
  • Recipe titles
  • Recipe URLs

4.2 No Sale of Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data with any third parties.

4.3 Google Services

If you choose to sign in to ratio.ai through the web interface, Google's privacy policy applies to the authentication process.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data during transmission and processing. Recipe data is processed server-side and not permanently stored unless you explicitly save recipes while logged in to ratio.ai.

6. Data Retention

  • Processing Data: Recipe content sent for processing is not permanently stored unless you save the recipe while logged in
  • Local Storage: Extension preferences and recent URLs are stored locally on your device until you uninstall the extension
  • Saved Recipes: If you save recipes while logged in to ratio.ai, they are stored according to our main privacy policy

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Uninstall the extension at any time to stop data collection
  • Clear extension storage through Chrome's extension settings
  • Contact us to request deletion of any saved data

8. Permissions Explanation

  • activeTab: Access current recipe page content when you click the extension
  • contextMenus: Add "Extract Recipe" option to right-click menu
  • scripting: Inject scripts to extract recipe data from websites
  • storage: Save your preferences and recent URLs locally
  • host permissions: Access recipe content from any cooking website

9. Children's Privacy

Our extension is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
